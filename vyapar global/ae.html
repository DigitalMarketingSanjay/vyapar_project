What is accounting software?

Accounting software is a computer program that assists bookkeepers and accountants in recording and processing the financial transactions of a business.

What is cloud accounting software?

Cloud accounting software is the accounting software that is hosted on remote servers, similar to the SaaS (Software as a Service) business model. It needs an active internet connection to operate.

What is a proforma invoice?

A proforma invoice is an estimate of the cost of a product or service. This can be provided to a buyer before the actual sale.

What is a credit note?

A credit note is a receipt given by a shop to a customer who has returned goods, which can be offset against future purchases.

How to make a tax invoice?

A tax invoice is a document that contains the details of applicable taxes on commodities along with product/ service, pricing, quantity, supplier and other details necessary to mention. These taxes may be VAT, service tax and other taxes.

How to make a bill of materials?

What is VAT Reverse Charge Mechanism?

The mechanism in which the end-user pays the VAT to the government instead of a seller. This happens when the seller is a non-tax paying entity.
