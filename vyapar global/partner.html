The use of VAT-compliant software assists in tracking VAT-related transactions, computing VAT obligations, and producing reports. Besides, companies should take into account other critical features like invoicing, inventory management, and financial reporting. By using Vyapar, businesses can efficiently manage their VAT obligations, generate accurate reports, and streamline their accounting processes, ultimately enabling them to focus on their core business operations.

Get Invoices in Arabic

Vyapar offers an Arabic invoice format suitable for businesses operating in GCC countries. The software enables businesses to create and customize invoices in Arabic to comply with VAT regulations. By using Vyapar's Arabic invoice format, businesses can ensure that they generate accurate invoices that meet the required standards for VAT compliance, regardless of their location within the GCC.

QR code on Invoices

As a VAT-compliant accounting software, Vyapar simplifies this process by allowing businesses to generate invoices with a built-in QR code, which customers can easily scan and verify through the ZATCA app. The QR code is a two-dimensional barcode that can be scanned using a smartphone or other mobile device. It contains essential information such as the invoice number, date, and amount, allowing customers and tax authorities to verify the authenticity of the invoice quickly. Implementing a QR code on invoices can help businesses streamline their invoicing process and comply with local regulations.
